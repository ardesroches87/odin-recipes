Okonomiyaki

Description

Okonomiyaki is a savoury Japanese pancake stuffed with yummy goodness! The ingredients provided are the basic okonomiyaki recipe. Feel free to add any vegetables or other meats. Have fun playing with the ingredients!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2/3 cup water
  • 4 cups chopped cabbage
  • 6 strips of bacon
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 sausage, diced (optional)
  • 1/2 cup green onions
  • 1/2 cup cooked shrimp (optional)
  • 1/4 cup tenkasu (optional)
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 tbsp Okonomiyaki sauce
  • 1 tsp mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Mix flour and water in a bowl until smooth. Stir in cabbage, bacon, eggs, sausage, green onions, shrimp, and tenkasu.
  2. Preheat a griddle or pan to 400 degrees F and coat with oil.
  3. Pour 1/4 of the batter onto preheated surface. Cook until golden brown, about 6 minutes per side. Transfer to a serving plate. Repeat to make a total of 4 pancakes.
  4. Drizzle Okonomiyaki sauce and mayonnaise over pancakes. Serve hot.
