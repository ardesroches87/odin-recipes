Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp milk (or water)
  • 1 tbsps of butter
  • salt and pepper (to taste)
  • chopped fresh chives (optional)

Steps

  1. Add eggs and milk to a bowl and beat with a fork or whisk for about 30 seconds.
  2. Heat up a pan to medium heat, then add butter.
  3. Once butter has melted, pour eggs into pan and immediately start stirring.
  4. Continue stirring eggs until just cooked.
  5. Immediately remove from hot pan and plate eggs on a plate.
  6. Add salt, pepper, and chives to taste. Enjoy!
