Ingredients

  • 1 box of Kraft Dinner
  • 6 cups of water
  • 2 tbsps of butter (or margarine)
  • 2 tbsps of milk
  • cracked black pepper (to taste)

Steps

  1. Add water to a medium sized pot and bring to a boil.
  2. Add pasta from Kraft Dinner box to boiling water (be careful not to add the cheese packet).
  3. Boil until pasta is cooked, about 6 minutes.
  4. Drain pasta (do not rinse).
  5. Return drained pasta to pot and add butter, milk, and cheese packet.
  6. Stir until combined. Add cracked pepper to taste. Serve immediately.
